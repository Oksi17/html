About Alan Alda

Childhood

Alda was born Alphonso Joseph D'Abruzzo on January 28, 1936, in the Bronx, New York City.
During Alda's junior year, he studied in Paris, acted in a play in Rome, and performed with his father on television in Amsterdam.
His father was of Italian descent and his mother was of Irish ancestry.

Career

Alda began his career in the 1950s.He has appeared in a number of TV series, game shows, and feature films, including a couple of Woody Allen movies.
He is best known for playing ‘Hawkeye Pierce’ in the long-running television series 'M*A*S*H.'
